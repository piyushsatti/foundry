# Writing

**Pages are SHORT.** AI generates content here; this contract keeps it human-followable. When in doubt, cut.

## The contract

- **BLUF.** Open with ONE sentence saying what the page is, then status. A reader who stops after line 1 still got the point.
- **~2-minute read cap** (soft). Past it, split into another file in the same folder — see [organization.md](organization.md).
- **Short sections, each with one job.** Lead sentences in **bold**.
- **Tables and lists beat paragraphs** for anything comparative or enumerable.
- **Link, don't repeat.** One fact has one home; everything else links to it.

## Cut

- Hedging and throat-clearing: "it's worth noting that", "in order to", "leverage".
- AI filler and abstraction. **Concrete over abstract.**
- Anything a diagram or table already says in prose (and vice versa).
