## 2026-07-05 — Round 4 geometry rulings (orchestrator-ratified; Pi verdict at renders)
Mock D's merging lines outran the round-3 spec (built for parallel lanes + reference links); five rulings resolve it, baked into `design/claude-design-iteration-4.md` §1: (1) **bullets/hues attach to LINES, not topics** — lines carry, topics group (line M crosses two topics; topic n08 lives entirely on line R); (2) **fork/merge runs are line strokes, not connectors** — termination at the target dot is the direction cue, no arrowheads at rest; merge targets wear the interchange ring; (3) **wire-hops promoted to every density a run renders at** (the one deviation from the noise audit, forced: at MAP no headings exist to kill a false touches-this-lane read); (4) **in-place merge landings carry no dot ornament at MAP** (rings stay cut) — the returning stroke, the OUTLINE tally chip, and the rail mark carry it; (5) **rail threads follow lines and never merge** — merging is map geometry; a thread simply ends when its line lands. Also standing: OUTLINE is home in all three candidates (MAP-as-home would need a law-1 amendment — noted, not made); node detail = side panel, modal on veto.

## 2026-07-05 — Round 5 direction: the map must carry a story (orchestrator, from 3-stream research convergence; Pi verdict at renders)
Three independent streams (games research, software research, narrative diagnosis) converged: **(1) geometry cannot narrate** — Shahaf et al.'s Information Cartography (found independently by BOTH research agents) built coherent metro lines that were "coherent, [but] not important"; **(2) lines must be cut editorially** — each line a narrative thread aimed at one question, labeled in words; mock-d's mechanically-derived lines (agents/files) are the root-cause candidate; **(3) words attach to structure at three altitudes** — goal/thesis banner with fill-in slots, line legends + beat-titled stations, docked generated synopsis whose sentences cross-highlight map elements; **(4) outputs are first-class** (Dagster assets → artifact shelf / terminal stations); **(5) churn compresses by curation into titled beats** ("less of an audit trail, more of a story"), key-moments-only as the default density; **(6) drill-in = semantic zoom in place**, one camera. **Adopted as schema v2** (from diagnosis ranking): versioned `session.intent` · `artifacts[]` · `session.frontier` · per-line `label`+`because` (editorial) · `beats[]` · `settled:` churn reframe · decision `standing` · node `moment` classification (breakthrough/setback/pivot/grind); rejected: per-node blanket reason, turn stratum, event digest. **Open question elevated to Pi (settled by renders, not text): map-primary vs prose-primary** — agent-trace vendors regressed to text views and incident.io's artifact of record is prose with the timeline as evidence; round 5 renders BOTH orientations.
